Who We Are

Our story, our mission, our promise.

With over a decade of experience, we craft exceptional safari experiences that connect you with Tanzania's incredible wildlife, landscapes, and cultures.

Your Trusted Tanzania Safari Experts

Founded in 2010, Blue Eagle Safaris has established itself as a premier provider of luxury safari experiences throughout Tanzania's most iconic destinations. Our team combines deep local knowledge with a passion for conservation and sustainable tourism.

We take pride in our personalized approach, creating tailor-made itineraries that cater to your specific interests and needs. Whether you're seeking the thrill of the Great Migration, the serenity of secluded camps, or immersive cultural experiences, our expert guides and planners will craft your perfect adventure.
